 👩‍💻 Stack Overflow Survey Analysis  

Tools: Python (Pandas, NumPy)  

📌 Project Overview  
Conducted an in-depth exploratory analysis of survey respondents to evaluate their professional background and demographic characteristics. The study focused on work experience, salary levels, country of origin, programming languages used, and education level, aiming to assess respondents from multiple analytical perspectives.

 🔎 Key Responsibilities  
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Calculation of aggregated metrics and summary statistics  
- Segmentation analysis by experience, salary, education, and geography  

 📈 Results  
- Identified that the average professional experience among respondents is **13 years**  
- Determined that **22%** of participants obtained their IT education through online courses  
- Concluded that professional success indicators showed no strong dependence on formal education level or country of origin
